How to Measure

  1. Place a piece of paper on the floor next to a wall.
  2. Have your child stand on the paper with heel against the wall.
  3. Mark the longest part of their foot on the paper with a pencil.
  4. Measure the distance from the wall to the mark.
Pro-Tip: If between sizes, choose the larger size for room to grow.
